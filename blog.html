The changing face of Retail Theft in Johannesburg
Beating the tag system.

Retail crime, theft and shoplifting is reportedly on the rise in Johannesburg, with criminals becoming far more savvy about beating the tag security system. They cut the tags out in-store so that the garments can be carried past the tag sensor system at the door without detection. Restaurants being targeted Another syndicate is also focusing on restaurants; 3 or 4 women are stealing handbags and wallets from restaurant tables and chairs.
